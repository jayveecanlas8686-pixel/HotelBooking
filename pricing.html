Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a commission on bookings?

Free plan has a 5% commission. Pro and Enterprise plans have 2% and 0% respectively.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Yes, all paid plans are month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel anytime from your dashboard.

How do I receive guest payments?

Payments are processed through our secure platform and transferred to your bank account within 3-5 business days after guest check-in.

Is this platform a real service?

StayEase is a portfolio/demo project built with static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All data is simulated using localStorage. It is not a real booking service.
